Dangerously Yours

A Vintage Romance Script

RudolphI'm going to tell you something tonight Catherine, something that will put my life in your hands

CatherineLife?

RudolphIt would mean my life, would the news to get certain circles

CatherineYes…

RudolphI love you, and I believe you love me!

CatherineYou’re quite wrong, this has been only an adventure to me!

RudolphThat isn’t true Catherine

CatherineIt is true! You smiled at me, I was flattered! It was an adventure for a holiday movie

RudolphYou may as well take my heart,Catherine. It is already full of you! You walked into it the day we met!

CatherineYou’re a fool, Rudolph

RudolphOh ho, but isn’t any man who falls in love… Do you know what you are to me? You are something to believe in again. You’re the type of person that had ceased to exist for me! A fine and honest woman!

CatherineOh my darling, you are such a child. Take your foolish little dream, and your heart, and go! Please go!

RudolphWhat is it, what’s wrong my dear?

CatherineYou know nothing about me, you’ve known me only 3 weeks!

Rudolph3 weeks?...Catherine I’ve known you all my life!

CatherineAll your life?

RudolphIt’s true! I’ve seen you in a thousand plays! Read you in as many books! Well, I’ve heard beautiful music and I thought, ‘she’d like that’! I’ve looked at flowers and known that one day, I’d give them to you!

CatherineOh stop, stop that! You must listen to me! I am not that woman! Perhaps I was once, but I am not now! You see, you were wrong! You can’t trust me

RudolphAre you trying to tell me that someone who’s name we both know and not mention, sent you?

CatherineWhat are you saying?

RudolphYou see, I’ve known all along. I had Arbear look you up the day you arrived.

CatherineAnd it- it didn’t make any difference?

RudolphIt didn’t make any difference. You see, I trust you. You came here to betray me…and to betray my country. That is your mission, Countess Rishula, and yet I am so sure of your love that I will trust you with my life and… what is far more valuable than the life of my own, the life of my country.

CatherineI will betray you.

RudolphIf you do, you will betray yourself at the same time.

CatherineYes, yes I know.

RudolphSo? You shall know my secret…Even now, as we sit here, there is a great network growing tighter around the foremost nations of the world. Treaties, pacts, alliances being formed against the man who sent you. Tonight, I will sign a pact that will set the wheels in motion to destroy him

CatherineDestroy him?! No!

RudolphHe’s made a great many mistakes! The greatest of all was sending you here, Catherine

CatherineWhy?!

RudolphHe knew I should love you, but he did not guess that you…would fall in love with me.

CatherineNo…He didn’t guess that

RudolphAnd you do see that you cannot betray him

CatherineIf I betray you, I betray myself…If I betray him, I betray my country. My country is very dear to me.

RudolphDearer than I?

CatherineNo, not dearer than you.

RudolphThen, will you help me defeat him?

CatherineHelp you, defeat him?

RudolphBy telling me his plans, that’s the only way I could hope to defeat him! We can’t both win. You’ll see, Catherine.

CatherineI’m… beginning to see…

RudolphThen, you will help me.

CatherineBy giving you any information I may possess, concerning our plans?

RudolphYes.

Catherine(sigh)You’re very clever, aren’t you? Oh, I can read you like a book now! You thought I was young and easily swayed! That you could make me love you, and that I would throw over my country, my duty for you?

RudolphThat is not the way to look at it, Catherine.

CatherineYou weren’t so wise after all! Because you’ve lost, you hear me?! Lost! You guessed wrong in our little duel of wits! You forgot how close hate is to love!

RudolphYou don’t know what you’re saying, Catherine

CatherineYou never loved me! You knew that I loved you, and you used that!

RudolphCatherine, stop talking like a child! We're playing for countries now!

CatherineYes we are! Aren’t we?...This is a gun in my hand Rudolph, I’d advise you to be careful of what you say.

RudolphWell, rather melodramatic, aren’t you? Tell me, will I be the seventh notch on the gun or the eighth? (laugh) Do you mind if I smoke?

CatherineSmoke?

RudolphI always smoke at the theater. Somehow, it enhances the performance.

CatherineYou can do anything you please, Rudolph. But you have very little time to do it in.

RudolphYou mean you’re actually going to kill me?

CatherineI mean just that.

RudolphWell, go ahead. I’ll do this my own way.

CatherineLook,you already know my purpose in being here. Now you will either give me my information or I will kill you. You have till nine o clock

RudolphYou won’t do it. You can’t pull the trigger. You can’t pull it because you love me. It takes a very brave and a very cold woman to do that, Catherine. I don’t think you can, isn't that true? Isn’t that why you're waiting?

CatherineThat’s not true!

RudolphOr is that you want to watch your victim? You want my heart constricted with agony, my hands to shake, you want me to plead for my life. So you can make a generous gesture and spare me! Sorry, Catherine, I don’t seem to be in the mood for prayers tonight.

CatherineYou don’t think I’ll do it? That's why you're so brave. You don't think I’ll do it. You wouldn’t be so brave otherwise! You’re a coward at heart! You lied to me, you deceived me!

RudolphYou tried to deceive me.

CatherineI’m tired of listening to you!

RudolphYou gave me your heart, you know. You’d like me to hand it back to you, whole again. But I won’t. You’ll live a long time yet, Catherine. An eternity without me, you will look into the faces of passersby hoping for something that will for an instant, bring me back to you. You will find moonlit nights strangely empty because when you call my name through them there will be no answer. Always your heart will be aching for me, and your mind will give you the doubtful consolation that you did, a brave thing.

CatherineYou dare to talk of bravery?!

RudolphWhat else do we have to talk about, Catherine? For me, there will never be another woman but you. But for my heart, there is another love that must come before you…my country. You're so still, your face is like ice. What are you thinking, Catherine?

CatherineWhat does anything you can say, matter? You’ve betrayed me in words,what good are words, when your heart is breaking? If I fail now, I should deserve to die. You tricked me into loving you Aren’t you forgetting that you came here for the same purpose?

RudolphI couldn’t betray you, I tried to tell you that, but you said you already knew. I was as honest as I knew how to be You think I wanted to love you? Knowing where you came from and what your mission was? Don’t you suppose that very hour we were together I was thinking, ‘she’s just pretending.’

CatherineI wasn’t! I loved you

RudolphAnd I loved you so much I let you pretend

CatherineYour time is up

RudolphThen my last words…I love you Catherine.

CatherineYou’re determined to die with a lie on your lips?!

(gun shoots)

RudolphI…love you, Catherine. Catherine Oh god.
